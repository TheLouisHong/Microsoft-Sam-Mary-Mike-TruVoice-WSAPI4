Demo DirectSound Audio Destination

The MultiVce.exe application demonstrates sending text-to-speech output to a DirectSound object using the DirectSound audio destination object.

Run the demo (MultiVce.exe). Select three voices you wish to have speak. Press OK.

Type in some long text into the box and press the "Speak #1" button. Type in different long text, and press the "Speak #2" button, and so on. Because DirectSound automatically mixes the audio channels together, the three voices will speak at the same time if you rapidly press one button after the other.

The "Use 3D sound" switch will attempt to use the sound card's 3D sound functionality if available. It may fail silently, either because the sound card doesn't support the functionality, or because Direct Sound 5.0 (or better) is not installed. If it's supported by the sound card, and you have Direct Sound 5.0 (or better) installed, you'll hear one voice from your left, one from center, and one from your right.
