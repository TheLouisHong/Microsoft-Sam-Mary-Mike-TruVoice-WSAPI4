Register for Updates

If you're interested in receiving future notifications about (proposed) changes and additions to the API, conferences, or new technologies, then please register.

When you press "Register now!", a blank E-mail form will appear. In the subject line, please write "Register" followed by one or more of the following keywords, separated by commas:

  • developer - Your primary interests are developing shipping applications with the Speech SDK.
  • engine vendor - You produce a speech recognition or text-to-speech engine that complies with the Microsoft Speech SDK.
  • university - You are affiliated with a university or other educational institution, and are using speech technology in your classes or research.
  • user - You are an end user who is interested in using speech recognition or text-to-speech in your applications.
  • recognition - You are interested in speech recognition.
  • synthesis - You are interested in text-to-speech
  • C++ - You develop in C++.
  • Java - You develop in Java.
  • Visual Basic - You develop in Visual Basic.

Example: A subject line might contain, "Register: developer, C++, recognition".
